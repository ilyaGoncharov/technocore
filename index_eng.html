About




Privately-held corporation “TehnoKor”, founded in 1997, provides development and manufacture of сapacitive type molecular electrical accumulators, means of pre ignition warm-up and ignition of internal-combustion engine (incl. gas-turbine engine), means of maintenance and repair of machinery.
